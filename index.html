About me
Can Nguyen Bao Chau or Chold is a girl who found her enthusiasm for visual field during her days at highschool. She joined many clubs and projects as a member of Media department. By the age of 17, she have determined to further her study in graphic design and she was offered scholarship at FPT University.

Fast forward to the present, she is freshman majoring in Digital Art and Design. She has a dream of becoming a UI/UX designer, providing the most aesthetic and relevant experiences to users by her work.
